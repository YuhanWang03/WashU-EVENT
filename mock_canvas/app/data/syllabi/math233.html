MATH 233 — Calculus III

Washington University in St. Louis · Summer 2026

A study of multivariable calculus: vectors and the geometry of space, partial derivatives, multiple integrals, and vector calculus through Green's Theorem. Weekly problem sets alternate between written submissions and WebAssign.

Grading

Problem sets 40%, quizzes 15%, midterm 20%, final exam 25%. The midterm is held in week 6.
