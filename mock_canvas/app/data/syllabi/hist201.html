HIST 201 — World History Since 1500

Washington University in St. Louis · Summer 2026

A global survey from 1500 to the present: the Columbian Exchange, empires and revolutions, the Enlightenment, industrialization, and the making of the modern world. Work centers on primary-source analysis and a document-based essay.

Grading

Primary source analyses 40%, document-based essay 20%, discussions 15%, final exam 25%.
